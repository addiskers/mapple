About Credit Loan Canada:

Credit Loan Canada offers a fast and simple loan search for borrowers all across Canada. You can apply for loans from $100 to $15,000 over 1 to 60 months.

Credit Loan Canada is not a direct lender, but we have a wide network of direct lenders. Credit Loan Canada provides a loan connection service to help you find a suitable lender based on your needs and circumstances. Credit Loan Canada's partners are all authorized, legitimate and reputable lenders, so you are in good hands.

You answer a few questions about yourself in our short loan application form, and we will search our wide network of Canadian lenders to find you a suitable loan offer. This way, your chances of approval are higher than going direct lender to lender.

Complete our online loan request form now, and get a super quick decision-it means that after submitting your application, we will process it real fast, and you will be presented with our suggestion for your top lending option on your screen in seconds.

You can proceed with the loan offer if you are happy with it, or you can decide not to. There is no obligation to continue.

If you proceed with the lender, they may carry out further checks with your approval. Successful applicants can have their funds deposited into their checking account within 1 hour, 24 hours, or the next business day (subject to lender processes).

What is Credit Loan Canada?

At Credit Loan Canada, we make it easy for consumers to reach out for a shorter-duration loan. We provide a website optimized for both desktop and mobile use. Whether you are at home, work, or on the train, you should have no trouble making a loan request through our site.

Credit Loan Canada is not a lending company. We do not offer loans. We do not broker online loans to lenders. No credit decisions are made on Credit Loan Canada. Instead, we work with a large network of reputable Canadian lenders. When you fill out our online form, we try to connect you with one of these lenders. If connected, you will be able to continue toward your goal of receiving a loan. It's our job to find you a lender. We offer this service free of charge.

Credit Loan Canada is Canada's leading loan comparison platform and our goal is to provide you with information to help you find the best products, the best providers and the best rates. We encourage you to read our privacy policy to learn more about what happens with your data when you submit a request for a product on our website.
