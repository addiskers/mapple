What is Credit Loan Canada?

Credit Loan Canada is not a lender, a loan broker, a financial institution or an agent of a loan broker or lender. Credit Loan Canada provides a no-obligation, free of charge service introducing prospective borrowers to prospective lenders, you can get multiple personalized loan offers from different personal loan lenders.

How much money can I borrow?

Credit Loan Canada's lending partners offer loans that range from $100-$15,000. The loan amount you qualify for can depend on which lender approves you, your credit profile, your net income and what province you live in.

What interest rate can I expect?

Rates and terms may vary by province and by lender. If we are successful in finding you loan offers, once you are connected with a lender, you will get the APR and fees applicable to you. All loans are subject to the lender's approval based on their own unique underwriting criteria.

What are the requirements to get a loan?

To be approved for a loan, you need to meet the individual requirements of the lender you are connected to. The lender will evaluate your information and decide if you are eligible based on its own established list of considerations such as income, past loan history and state of residence.

When you click on one of your loan offers, your chosen lender may ask for extra information in order to approve your loan. For the best chance of receiving loan offers, make sure you fill out the form with truthful and accurate information.

Can I apply even if I have bad credit?

Yes! We know that many Canadians face challenges in qualifying for loans and other services due to their credit score. However that does not mean no solutions exist. In fact, our platform helps Canadians to qualify for any loan regardless of their credit score based on our Lender's criteria.

Does Credit Loan Canada check my credit score?

No. If you apply for a service using our website, we only collect the minimum amount required for you to qualify with a provider but at no point does Credit Loan Canada check your credit score.

Will I be prequalified if I have bad credit?

Credit Loan Canada has partnerships with numerous loan lending partners to ensure we can offer loans and financial products to as many customers as possible. Some of the lenders we work with offer bad credit personal loans. The final decision always comes down to the lender, so while we cannot guarantee you will get a loan, we will always do our best to find suitable loan offers for you.

In any case, since our search does not affect your credit score, it is completely free of charge and no-obligation, you can give it a try and see if you have any loan offers available.

How much will my loan cost?

Your lender determines the cost of your loan. The lender also sets the fees and interest associated with your loan. This information appears in your loan agreement. We recommend reading through your loan agreement carefully before you sign it.

Is my personal and financial information secure?

Credit Loan Canada uses various security systems to maintain the safety of your personal information. All personal and sensitive data transmitted between your computer's browser and our website uses 128 bit Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption technology.

Please review our Terms and Conditions and Privacy Policy for comprehensive information regarding our loan service and the safety of your information.
